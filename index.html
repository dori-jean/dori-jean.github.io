Welcome!

I am happy your journey has led you here. I am a self-compassion advocate, using my creativity to awaken the light inside of us all, to inspire self-expression. Through my studies of the arts and yoga, I have learned about my truest self and my passion for helping others heal and grow into their highest potential. Love is contagious. The more we love ourselves the more we are able to love those around us. I combine the power of movement with the power of creativity, creating a balance of the mind, body and soul.

Hi y'all, I'm Dori Jean

I am a family first, southern grown, gal with an open heart that craves the connections I make with those on this journey with me. My roots are in Memphis, TN but my need for the energy of the ocean has landed me in Tampa, FL. I have always been passionate about the arts; the beat of a song, the feel of a brush stroke, the need for movement and self-expression are what pulses through my veins. I started dancing at the young age of 4 and realized I was better at expressing myself through my art than with my words. This path led me to receiving my Bachelor of Fine Arts in Performing Arts with a Dance emphasis.

After college, I followed my dreams to New York City where my passion for yoga and sharing it with others really took root. My dancing career began to take its toll on my body and the realization that I took it for granted began to weigh heavily on me. I needed a change and to listen to my body as well as my heart. I earned my Yoga Teacher Certification from Three Sisters Yoga and began my journey of self-compassion and healing. The pivotal point on this self discovery was when my husband and I followed our hearts down to the warm Florida sunshine. I believe in cosmic connections and those that come into our lives are ways for us to grow and learn about ourselves as well as them.

The next major shift on my journey was a special connection I made through yoga that led me to begin using Young Living products. It was an answer to my prayers, enhancing my yoga practice and my art while allowing me to live the toxic-free lifestyle I had craved for so many years.

Being a wife and mother, my focus is to not let the special in between moments get muddled by the natural everyday grind. I always knew there was a way to achieve my dreams and support my family and because of my determination and grit I carved out my path and have become a healer in my household. I accept change with open arms and with each new shift in my path I learn more about myself.

I am here to share my lifestyle through art, yoga, and essential oils to inspire and illuminate you to welcome the energies of abundance and balance into your life.
